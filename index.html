Foundation of Animation

It is a work of Frank Thompson and Ollie Jackson.
Principles of Animation :-
1. Squash and Stretch : tells about the stretchness of the material.
2. Anticipation : It acknowledges that all movement starts from a small movement in opposite direction.
3. Staging : It helps in communicating our messages to audiences better
4. Straight Ahead and Pose to Pose : It generally means planning about your animation before touching the computer.
5. follow Through and overlapping : It is about the loose items on our character as a hat.
